CMCCurated Music Collage

There is a truly overwhelming amount of recorded music in existence today--so much that it can be difficult even for a dedicated music fan to keep track of what they've heard or want to hear. RunCMC is an online community that curates a music collage topster, ignites music discovery, and provides a platform to discuss favorite albums and topsters.

Special thanks to Spotify for powering this application to give users the opportunity to search for their favorite artists and albums.
